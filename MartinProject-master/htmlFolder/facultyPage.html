Our Faculty

Our faculty is composed of distinguished US and Japanese business leaders, government officials, scientists and university professors who share an interest in energy security, climate, policies, economic progress and geo-political expertise.

They are presented in the training program with specific videos made for the US-Japan Energy Institute
