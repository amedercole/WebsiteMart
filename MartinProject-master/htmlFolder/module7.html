Consider These Questions While Reading The Documents and Watching The Videos

  • Siberian Pipeline—Economic War against Soviet Union (including NSSD 66). What might our adversaries be planning against us?
  • Energy Espionage—past experiences and future vulnerabilities.
  • Defining Economic Security: Williamsburg Summit Communique: 1983. What is the global economic outlook and Implicatons for electric power? What role can nuclear power play?
  • Middle East protection war plan (Iran-Iraq war). How do we protect oil/gas sea lanes against our top adversary, China?
  • Ending the Cold War —how do we confront current Cold War with China? How do we compete with Russia/China in exporting nuclear power plants?
  • Launching ITER — a plan of international cooperation; what further cooperation is needed for decarbonization and energy security on a global scale? Is Hydrogen a solution? Nuclear power: large scale and SMR? Fusion?
  • Nuclear power and Nuclear non-proliferation (the US-Japan nuclear cooperation agreement)…challenges confronting the world today. How do we showcase Rokkasho? Why is Rokkasho important to nuclear power?
  • Protecting vital infrastructure: — Abu Nidal terrorist airport attack. Potential attacks on vulnerable electrical infrastructure. What has Fukushima taught us about nuclear safety?
  • Martin-Trump North Korea — the importance of “What IF”. How do we deal with authoritative leaders in China, North Korea, Russia? What will be impact on energy security?
  • National Security calendar…from Reagan to Trump (unique Martin). How can we use the calendar for our own electric utility companies and within electric utilities planning for future growth in nuclear power?
