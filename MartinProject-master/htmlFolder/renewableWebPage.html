Be Our Guest

Welcome to Robinhood Farm, where history meets sustainability.
Founded in 1780, this remarkable farm has witnessed the passage of great figures like George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, and Lafayette, who named the nearby town Havre de Grace. Today, Robinhood Farm is on a mission to create a sustainable future through energy training.
